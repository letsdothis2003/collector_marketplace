📖 About OBTAINUM

The techno marketplace for collectors and hobbyists

🎯 Our Mission

OBTAINUM was born from a simple idea: create a fair marketplace where collectors can buy and sell without fear of scalping or unfair pricing. We believe in transparency, community, and the thrill of the hunt.

✨ What Makes Us Different

  • 🤖 AI-Powered Price Analysis - Our AI analyzes market value to help you make informed decisions
  • 🛡️ No Scalping Policy - We actively monitor and prevent price gouging
  • 💬 Direct Seller Communication - Chat directly with sellers, no middlemen
  • ⭐ Community Driven - Built by collectors, for collectors
  • 🔒 Secure Transactions - Your data and privacy are protected

📜 Our Story

Founded in 2024, OBTAINUM started as a passion project for vintage toy collectors who were tired of bots and scalpers ruining the hobby. Today, we've grown into a full-featured marketplace serving thousands of collectors across categories including electronics, collectibles, clothing, and more.

🌱 Future Plans

We're constantly improving! Upcoming features include: blockchain authentication for rare items, expanded AI recommendations, mobile apps, and community forums.
