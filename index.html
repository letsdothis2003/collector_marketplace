About OBTAINUM

We built OBTAINUM because we were tired of scalpers ruining the hobby. This is the marketplace we wished existed.

Our Mission

To build a community marketplace where collectors can buy and sell at fair, MSRP-anchored prices — free from bots, scalpers, and price gouging. Every listing is community-monitored.

Community First

OBTAINUM is powered by its community. Users can flag unfair listings, review sellers, and earn trust badges. Bad actors are removed fast — good sellers are celebrated.

AI-Powered Fairness

Our Gemini-powered AI scans every listing and compares prices to MSRP and market data, automatically flagging suspected scalp pricing before it harms buyers.

Buyer & Seller Protection

Every transaction is covered by our guarantee. Buyers get what they paid for, or they get their money back. Sellers are protected from fraudulent claims.

The OBTAINUM Code

  • List at or below MSRP whenever possible.
  • Describe your item honestly — disclose all flaws.
  • Respond to buyers within 24 hours.
  • Ship within 3 business days of payment.
  • No bot-purchased inventory.
  • No price gouging during shortages.
  • No fake listings or bait-and-switch.
