About OBTAINUM

We built OBTAINUM because we were tired of scalpers ruining the hobby. This is the marketplace we wished existed.

Our Mission

To build a community marketplace where collectors can buy and sell at fair, MSRP-anchored prices — free from bots, scalpers, and price gouging.

Community First

OBTAINUM is powered by its community. Users can flag unfair listings and earn trust badges. Bad actors are removed fast.

Buyer Protection

Every transaction is covered. Buyers get what they paid for, or they get their money back. Sellers are protected from fraudulent claims.

Market Fair

All prices are monitored against MSRP. Listings priced above MSRP are flagged as potential scalping. Community reviews everything.
